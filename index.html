Key Features

Our platform offers comprehensive solutions for both goods owners and transporters

Smart Matching

AI-powered algorithm matches shipments with the most suitable transporters based on location, capacity, and ratings.

Secure Escrow Payments

Integrated payment system holds funds in escrow until delivery is confirmed, protecting both parties.

Real-time Tracking

GPS-enabled tracking allows goods owners to monitor their shipments in real-time throughout the journey.

Digital Documentation

All contracts, waybills, and delivery confirmations are handled digitally for efficiency and record-keeping.

Rating System

Comprehensive rating system builds trust within the community and ensures quality service.

Mobile First

Fully responsive design works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

How It Works

Simple and efficient process for both goods owners and transporters

Register Your Account

Sign up as either a goods owner or transporter with basic verification.

Create or Browse Listings

Goods owners post shipments, transporters browse available loads.

Match & Negotiate

Our algorithm suggests optimal matches, parties negotiate terms.

Secure Payment & Transport

Payment is secured in escrow, transport begins with tracking.

Confirm Delivery & Release Funds

Goods owner confirms delivery, funds are released to transporter.

Rate Experience

Both parties rate each other to build community trust.
